### What is Layout Density?
Layout density controls how much spacing and padding is used throughout the app.

### How to Change It
1. Go to **Settings > Appearance**
2. Scroll to the **Layout Density** section
3. Choose your preference:
   - **Compact**: More content on screen, less spacing
   - **Comfortable**: Balanced spacing (recommended)
   - **Spacious**: More breathing room, easier to read
4. Watch the live preview to see how it looks
5. The change applies automatically across the entire app
