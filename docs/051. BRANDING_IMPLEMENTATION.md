Throughout the application, the rocket/launch metaphor is reinforced:

- **Icon**: Rocket emoji/icon used consistently

- **Aspirational**: Suggests reaching new heights
- **Action-Oriented**: Launch implies taking off, moving forward
- **Memorable**: Strong visual metaphor
- **Professional**: Serious about career growth
- **Positive**: Exciting and optimistic
